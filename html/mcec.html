Me Casa Es Tu Casa

Legacy Project

MCEC is a Couchsurfer clone, where you can find a host who offers you a room anywhere in the world, this app gives you a great and safe experience on your travels while saving money . You have the opportunity to find a host during your holiday wherever is your destination.You can also join the community and become a host .

Technologies used

Angular : framework for building client applications using HTML and TypeScript combined with AngularMaterial and Bootstrap.

NodeJS : back-end JavaScript runtime environment.

PostgreSQL: Relational database management system for storage.

Express: Node.js web application framework.

Bcrypt and JWT: for authentication.

Cloudinary: image cloud storage.
